Our Story: Why We Are "Because Scrub"

Founded by a group of university students with an entrepreneurial spirit, our project was born from the desire to create a dedicated retail business focused on **medical apparel**. We specialize in providing high-quality, stylish scrubs for **doctors, nurses, dentists, and students** in these fields. Our focus is centered on exceptional **fabric quality, beautiful colors** that suit all ages and tastes, and designs tailored for every medical specialty, all while maintaining **accessible and competitive prices** for everyone.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality, most comfortable, and durable medical scrubs, empowering healthcare workers to feel confident and move freely while dedicating themselves to saving lives.

Our Values

  • *Quality: Uncompromising standards in fabric and stitching.
  • *Comfort: Designing for long shifts and constant motion.
  • *Style: Offering modern cuts and colors.
  • *Support: Dedicated to serving the healthcare community.

Thank you for choosing Because Scrub. We are honored to be a part of your essential work.
